Our Story

Parallel Lines Finally Cross

For years, our worlds moved in perfect parallel—always close, heading in the same direction, yet never quite touching.

2018

Arwin was wrapping up his college years at BSU just as Jen was stepping onto the very same campus to begin hers but ended up in PUP. By 2020, we were both walking into the Alorica building every day. Arwin spent his shifts on the 21st floor, while Jen worked on the 14th. We rode the same elevators and passed through the same hallways, yet somehow missed each other in the crowd.

2022

The universe tried again. We both transitioned to TaskUs. For months, we shared the same workplace as quiet colleagues, completely unaware that our soulmate was only a few desks away. Both of us carried the weight of long, failed relationships from our pasts. We were cautious, guarded, and quietly trying to learn how to trust again.

July 2023

It took a little liquid courage, a funny meme, and a reply to a MyDay photo for Arwin to finally break the silence and send that first message. What began as a late-night laugh quickly sparked a conversation that never stopped. Once our parallel lines finally crossed, the healing began. We found joy in the simplest moments—spontaneous food trips, cozy café dates, long scenic drives, and endless laughter. Together, we learned that love is not measured by the number of days, months, or years you’ve been together. Love is measured by how much you choose each other, every single day.

We went from strangers sharing the same buildings to partners sharing a lifetime.
